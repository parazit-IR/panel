# 17. Immediate versus scheduled notifications

Use two paths.

## Immediate event-driven enqueue

For events such as:

* receipt submitted;
* receipt rejected;
* Payment approved;
* provisioning completed;
* provisioning failed;
* token rotated;
* Subscription revoked.

The business transaction may create a Notification row through a transactional outbox-style enqueue.

Requirements:

* Notification row created in same local transaction as the relevant state transition where practical;
* Telegram send occurs later;
* no direct Telegram send inside business transaction;
* repeated event creates one notification by notificationKey.

## Scheduled candidate scanning

For time/threshold notifications:

* Payment approaching expiry;
* Subscription approaching expiry;
* traffic low/exhausted.

Use Task 38 scheduled jobs to create Notification rows.

Do not send directly from candidate scanner.

# 33. Delivery transaction phases

## Phase A — Claim

Short transaction:

* select row;
* status CLAIMED/SENDING;
* attempt count;
* commit.

## Phase B — Resolve/render/send

Outside transaction:

* load safe read models as needed;
* relevance check may use short independent transactions;
* render;
* Telegram send.

## Phase C — Complete

Short transaction:

* SENT with Telegram message ID;
* or FAILED/UNKNOWN/DEAD/CANCELLED;
* schedule retry.

Do not annotate the whole processor with one transaction.

# 34. Telegram delivery uncertainty

Telegram sending is not exactly once.

A timeout may happen after Telegram accepted the message.

Policy:

* mark UNKNOWN;
* do not immediately resend credential or financial messages blindly;
* transactional reminders may tolerate a possible duplicate after bounded delay;
* use deliveryFingerprint;
* reconcile only where Telegram message ID/result is known;
* Telegram does not provide a universal client-defined idempotency key.

Recommended:

## Low-risk reminders

* UNKNOWN may retry once after delay;
* possible duplicate accepted.

## Sensitive security events

* avoid blind multiple retries;
* mark DEAD or require operational review after uncertainty threshold.

## Provisioning completed

* one conservative retry allowed.

Document limitations clearly.

Never claim exactly-once Telegram delivery.
